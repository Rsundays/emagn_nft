NFT - Non-Fungible Token

Even if NFT might sound like a new word, the reality is that NFTs exist since 2014 and according to data from market tracker DappRadar, Sales volumes of non-fungible tokens (NFTs) surged to $10.7 billion in the third quarter of 2021, which is one of the main reasons why they are gaining so much popularity.
An NFT is a digital asset that represents a unique tangible (and untangible) object like art, music, items in games or videos. NFTs can be bought and sold online and in the case of eMAGN, you can do it through one of the biggest platforms, OpeanSea.Check our upcoming section: How to Buy NFT.

When you purchase a NFT, you are generally acquiring a one of a kind, unique asset. This is only possible because each NFT contains distinguishing information that makes it both distinct from any other NFT and easily verifiable. It also contains built-in authentication which serves as a proof of ownership. Majority of NFTs are built using Ethereum blockchain that prevents modifying the records of ownership.
