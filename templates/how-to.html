Buy NFT in OpenSea

Before you can buy or sell NFTs, you need to create a Crypto Wallet where you will store the Ethereum you need in order to do the transactions. In our case, we are using MetaMask but you can use any other wallet like CoinBase.

  1. Create a Crypto Wallet with MetaMask.

    If you are installing MetaMask in your Desktop, we would recommend you to use Google Chrome, otherwise you can also download the app from Apple Store and Google Play Store.

    If you have any other queries about MetaMask, you can also check their FAQ here.

  2. Create a new Account.

    Creating a MetaMask account is free. There are a lot of tutorials out there so instead of re-inventing the wheel, we are just including one that we found online that describes each of the steps in a very clear and detailed way.
    Step-by-Step Guide.

  3. Add Funds to your Crypto Wallet.

    Adding funds to your wallet is like purchasing any other item online. Click on Buy and you will be presented with 2 options to purchase Ether, Transak and Wyre. Both will charge some network and exchange fee as part of the transaction, so we recommend you to read the information based on your country before you perform the operation to choose which one is better for you. At the time of writing this article, our preferred option was Wyre due to the transaction fee. Once you have chosen your method, finish the process and your wallet will be loaded with your new ETH funds.

  4. Buy (or sell) the NFT.

    Here we have 2 links to explain the very last step of the process:
    • Click here to read the documentation from OpenSea.
    • This is a very good video from Cora Harrison How To Buy An NFT on OpenSea - Crypto For Beginners
